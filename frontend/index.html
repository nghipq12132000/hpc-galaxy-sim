Galaxy Collision Physics

This simulation integrates Newton's Law of Universal Gravitation between all stars. Acceleration of star i is given by:

a_i = G * Σ [ (m_j * (r_j - r_i)) / (|r_j - r_i|^2 + ε^2)^(3/2) ]

where G = 6.674e-11, and ε is a softening factor to prevent numerical singularities during close star encounters.

MPI Communication Pattern

In the C++ MPI implementation, coordinates of local stars are shared across nodes using collective gathering:

MPI_Allgatherv(local_x, local_count, ...)

Every rank holds the full set of particle positions, dividing the force calculation loop into slices for perfect load-balancing.
